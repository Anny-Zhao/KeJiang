Welcome to HPMS Research Group

High-Performance Metallic Structure (HPMS) Research Group is led by Dr. Ke Jiang at University of Canterbury, New Zealand. Our group conducts research into high-performance metallic structures.

Research Areas

High-performance connections and members

Investigating innovative connection designs and structural members for optimal performance.

Anti-seismic Devices

Development and testing of devices to improve structural resistance to seismic events.

Structural Fire Engineering

Study of post-fire behaviour of steel and composite structures.

AI in Structures

Application of artificial intelligence in structural engineering.
